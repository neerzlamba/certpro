Why CertPro?

The story of how we are digitizing the education sector across the globe.

The Problem

For decades, educational certificates have been physical, fragile, and easily faked. This leads to costly background checks for employers and high friction for students trying to prove their merit.

Our Mission

CertPro was founded with one simple vision: to make every educational achievement instantly verifiable and 100% tamper-proof. We empower institutes with a professional, secure platform to issue digital credentials that students truly own.

How it Works

By leveraging advanced encryption and QR-based public verification, we ensure that a certificate issued by a college in Mumbai can be verified by an employer in London in under 2 seconds.
