How It Works

1️⃣

Student Request

Students request their digital certificates from their respective university portals.

2️⃣

Staff Review

University staff verify academic records and approve the request to the administrators.

3️⃣

Admin Issuance

Administrators authorize and issue the final, completely tamper-proof digital document.

4️⃣

Public Verification

Anyone can instantly verify the authenticity of the certificate using its unique ID.

Core Features

Instant Verification

A public portal allows any employer or organization to instantly verify a certificate to ensure it is valid and has not been forged.

Automated Processing

Easy to track digital system. Students submit requests, staff review them, and administrators approve them quickly all in one dashboard.

Secure Storage

All issued certificates are securely stored in the database. Every action, including approvals and tracking, is actively logged.

Role Management

Dashboards are visually separated. University Administrators only see their specific students, while Superadmins oversee the entire system.

Automated Setup

Instantly handles bulk university setups and student data imports out of the box, letting institutions get started immediately.

Analytics Dashboard

Real-time visual reports on every dashboard. Managers can easily track pending requests, student numbers, and certificate issuance stats.

User Roles

Super Admin

  • Oversees all system Universities
  • Add, Edit, Block Universities
  • Manage Courses & Certificate types
  • View complete system activity

University Admin

  • Manages their own University
  • Approves and issues certificates
  • Views localized university data
  • Manages their own staff members

Office Staff

  • Acts as the first review step
  • Reviews incoming student requests
  • Adds remarks or system notes
  • Forwards valid requests to Admins

Enrolled Student

  • Manages their personal profile
  • Requests new academic certificates
  • Gets shareable Verification Links
  • Tracks their request progress

Fast and Reliable

Built completely without heavy frameworks or complicated backends. This system operates natively on your browser using fast, structured data storage to guarantee instantaneous load speeds.
