FREQUENTLY_ASKED_QUESTIONS

How do colleges join CertPro?

Colleges can sign up directly from our portal. Once verified by our administrators, they are assigned a unique University ID to begin issuing digital certificates.

Are the certificates tamper-proof?

Yes. Every certificate is cryptographically signed. If a single character is changed, the QR verification will immediately flag the document as invalid.

How do students share their degrees?

Students can curate their "Career Profile" in our portal. They can choose which degrees to make public and then share their unique profile link with employers.

Is there a cost for verification?

Public verification via the web portal or QR scanner is completely free for employers and the general public.

What if I lose my login credentials?

Please contact your respective university administration office if you are a student or staff member. System managers can reset accounts via the global user manager.
